I'm a digitally led integrated designer, with over 20 years experience, I'm currently based in east London.

My career began as a digital designer in one of the first digital agencies in the UK, AMX digital, headed up by Malcolm Garrett. Working on projects for Univsersal Music, EMI, Barclays, Science Museum, ICA and D&AD.

Over the last 10 years Ive worked across online and offline integrated campaigns for clients such as Mercedes, Nivea Men, Cadburys, & Experience design for Peugeot/Citroen DS and The Electoral Commission.

I'm able to work solo with advertising teams or solo on art direction & design projects. My design work has been exhibited in Design Manchester exhibitions over the last 4 years. I'm also a printmaker and artist. My work was included in the Dora Marr open call exhibition at the Tate Modern and I'm represented by various galleries.
